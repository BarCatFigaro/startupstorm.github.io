What are we about?

Startups are very common nowadays — anyone with an idea can start their own company.



That is, if you are lucky enough to live in a tiny strip in California. Unfortunately, for the rest of the world, engineering and business students aren’t able to get mentorship, advice, and funding to spin their class projects into multimillion-dollar businesses as easily. This is the pain point we want to solve.

The mission behind StartupStorm is this: to empower students to make their own startups from idea to seed funding and everything in between and and two, to ignite the entrepreneurial and building spirit not only in Sauder and engineering but in the whole of UBC. In short, we’re taking UBC innovation by storm.
